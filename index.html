About SL Personal Training

Hi I'm Tyrone Walton, and I am the founder of SL Personal Training. We focuses on making optimal health a priority in your life. Sessions are lead by fitness coaches with extensive education and experience in the craft of conditioning the mind & body. We specialize in:

  • Interval Training (HIIT)

SL Personal Training specialists creatively make your workouts more exciting & effective. We provide the best quality of training to transform your body & meet your fitness goals.
